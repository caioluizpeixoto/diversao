# **App Name**: FaithPlay Adventures

## Core Features:

- Printable Bible Games: Offers a variety of 26 printable bible-themed games.
- Bonus Content: Includes bonus activities such as Bible challenges, coloring pages, and devotional planner, as a "tool" which the LLM decides if and when they can be used.
- Safe and Educational Content: Ensures all content is safe, educational, and aligned with Christian values.
- Easy-to-Use PDFs: Provides easy-to-download and print PDF files for all games and activities.
- Two-Tier Offer: Offers the base level pack of games, and a premium package with bonus activities to parents to purchase
- Customer Feedback System: Gathers customer testimonials

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Gentle blue (#A0CFEC) to evoke a sense of calm and faith.
- Background color: Very light, desaturated blue (#F0F8FF).
- Accent color: Soft yellow (#F7E7CE) to add warmth and a touch of childlike wonder.
- Headline font: 'Belleza' sans-serif for headings, paired with 'Alegreya' serif for body text; Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- Use simple, friendly icons related to biblical themes.
- Clean, well-spaced layout with clear sections and CTAs.  Two cards for tiers of access with CTAs, plus guarantee boxes. Ensure the site is accessible on all devices.
- Subtle animations and transitions to keep users engaged.
